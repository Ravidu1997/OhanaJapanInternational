Japan



Japan is the eleventh most populous country in the world, as well as one of the most densely populated and urbanized. As a leading Sri Lankan agency specializing in international employment, we take pride in connecting skilled and dedicated Sri Lankan employees with reputable organizations in Japan, including the Organizational Technical Intern (OTIT) Programs and Specified Skilled Worker (SSW) programs. We understand the significance of global work experience, and our mission is to empower Sri Lankan talents by facilitating their employment in Japan's thriving job market.

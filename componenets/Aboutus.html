About Us

Sri Lankan skilled workers are dispatched to good job opportunities in foreign countries. We help them good paying job opportunities abroad. We are doing all these activities in association with the Sri Lanka Bureau of foreign employment. To find out the qualifications of the foreign job seekers for the respective job inquire into the personal background of the prospect and verify the authenticity-related information.

We exchange true information of job candidates with our foreign agents in the respective countries. All foreign job candidates who come to us are given extensive knowledge about the relevant foreign countries as well as job opportunities.

We refer the unskilled workers who come to us to the recognized training institutes in Sri Lanka for suitable job opportunities and give the necessary advice. We are currently dispatching workers to European countries and Japan. Japan has opened employment gate for Sri Lankan youth under the job opportunities given to many countries. We are contributing to the OTIT and SSW employment programs by operating in Sri Lanka.
